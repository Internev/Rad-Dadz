Rad-Dadz is the internet's primary resource for Dads vainly trying to remain cool.

At Rad-Dadz we understand the existential angst of "cool" passing you by. We provide an online location you can visit to interact with others like yourself: people who get what's actually cool. Yep, we're talking comfortable sweatpants, jorts, socks with sandals, and simple groan-inducing puns.

We also provide youth-translation services, including acronyms and emoji!
